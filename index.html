Our Story

Built on Inheritance; Not Religion.

The Inheritance Nation was born from a simple revelation: you are not a sinner saved by grace — you are a king restored to the throne. Too many people live beneath their identity, trapped by shame, confusion, and purposelessness. We exist to end that.

Our spiritual philosophy is rooted in three unshakeable truths: Inheritance — everything the Father has belongs to you. Sonship — you are not a servant begging for crumbs; you are a child seated at the table. Dominion — you were designed to influence, lead, and transform every space you occupy.

"The Home of Kings" isn't a title we gave ourselves — it's the identity we help you discover. Kings aren't born in palaces. They're forged in purpose, refined by truth, and crowned by the One who called them. That's what happens here.

Our vision? To raise a generation of individuals, families, and nation-builders who live from heaven's blueprint — not the world's template.

"You are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people."

— 1 Peter 2:9

Our Inheritance Confession

The Inheritance Nation has now become a ministry known by all men. Just like a shelter from the wind, we are preserving men from chaos and confusion, like a refuge from the storm, we are saving many from their troubles, pains and sorrows, and like a stream of water in a dry place we bring many into hope, restoration, peace and prosperity.

This is now a house of light bringing wisdom, knowledge and understanding, innovation, inspiration and illumination to many. This is now like a city set upon the hill, receiving the attention of many.

Multitudes of people have joined this ministry to worship God and to learn of Christ, powerful and influential men have also come submitting themselves to be discipled, to learn of Christ and to worship Him.

By this we have now become an enviable family, a mighty force on the earth, a ministry with excess resources for ministry work, a convergence of influential people growing in influence, in affluence, in wealth and in riches. We are now impacting many lives around the world, with the gospel of Christ, turning sinners to saints and saints to disciples, bringing truth and healings to the Nations of the world.

We are known for God's mighty power, wealth and riches, glory and beauty, love and honor, wisdom and truth, strength and audacity, and every of God's blessing.

The Inheritance Nation is God's dwelling place, meeting men's need and raising men who meet the needs of humanity. A home of kings, making kings out of mere men.

We have become the most enviable people on the earth, eating the wealth of the nations, conquering every obstacles, breaking new grounds for the kingdom and expanding across the world.

We have now become so many that we cannot be ignored, a strong force on the earth, compelling men to serve the living God. Great men are constantly coming to us bringing their wealth and riches to serve the living God and honor His name.

We are kings on earth radiating God's love and righteousness, heading for greater glory in heaven with Christ.

Pre-Sermon Confession

Today I lay aside all distractions and concerns weighing down my soul and I receive with meekness the word of God that saves my soul and conforms me to the image of Christ.

I listen under the influence of the Holy Spirit, my soul is void of distractions, burdens and cares. As I look into and hear the scriptures being read, I receive inspiration, sound doctrine, reproof, correction, and instruction in righteousness, being made ready for life and destiny.

My face is unveiled and as I look at the scriptures, I see the face of Jesus and as I see His face, I become more like Him.

As the word comes, it renews me, builds me up and by it I am led into my inheritance. Amen.

Confession for Giving

Tithes, Offerings & Seeds

Lord out of the abundance that you have given I bring back my offerings to you.

I recognize your Lordship over my life and my goods and with love in my heart I have brought this offering to honor your name.

By this offering I increase in grace for more wealth and riches and for every charitable works.

Thank you Lord Jesus for receiving my offerings today. Amen.

Confession: Light of Christ

The Spirit of Christ lives on the inside of me and He has flooded my heart with His light. This light shines into every compartment of my life extinguishing every form of darkness and producing wisdom, knowledge and understanding.

By this wisdom I navigate the paths of life and I have firm footing.

I have the light of God resident on the inside of me. I know what is good for me for the Lord shows me the best pathway to my destiny.

The Lord is my shepherd, I'm a sheep of His pasture, I know His voice and I heed His voice. He leads me in the right path where I find rest and fulfillment. The voice of the stranger I do not heed.

I have believed in Jesus the one who is light, I do not walk in darkness. He is the fountain of God's life bubbling up in me, the light by which I see.

I am blessed with my teachers and they enhance my light. I hear the voice of the Holy Spirit telling me which way to go, what to do and how to do it.

I receive His directions daily and I am empowered to follow.

I have the mind of Christ, I think His thoughts and I speak His words.

I know the things freely given to me. I am never distracted from God's purpose for my life, not chasing frivolities but only reaching for the destiny and inheritance He has bought for me.

God is light, He lives in me, and I am light.
